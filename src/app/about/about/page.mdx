Welcome to **AccessFinder**, a web application created to help people with disabilities find places and services that are accessible to their needs!

## Who We Are

Access Finder is currently developed and maintained by me (Naya Singhania), a disabled college student studying computer science.
With Access Finder, I hope to be able to use my technical skills to foster accessibility in communities.

## What does this app do?

Access Finder is a web application that allows users to search for places and services based on their accessibility needs.
Users can see a variety accessibility indicators that are generated from reviews by users. and make decisions of where to go.
Users can also rate their accessibility experiences at a location to help others in the community.
