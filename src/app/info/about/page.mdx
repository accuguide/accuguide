Welcome to **Accuguide**, a web application created to help people with disabilities find and rate places based on various accessibility features!

## Who We Are

Accuguide is currently developed and maintained by me (Naya Singhania), a disabled college student studying computer science, as well as various open-source contributors who work on small improvements.
As Accuguide grows, we hope to add more people to our team!

## What does this app do?

Accuguide is a web application that allows users to search for places and services based on their accessibility needs.
Users can see a variety accessibility indicators and make decisions of where to go.
Users can also rate their accessibility experiences at a location to help others in the community know what places are accessible and what places to avoid.
