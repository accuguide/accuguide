<Title>Frequently Asked Questions</Title>

Here you can find answers to some of the most common questions about Access Finder. Please reach out to us via email if you have any other questions or need further assistance.

## Why is my profile picture a link?

We currently do not have the capacity to store user's profile pictures. Instead we are able to use links to images as your profile picture. Upon first login, your profile picture will be your Google Account photo.

## Is my data safe?

We take your privacy and security very seriously. We only save the personal data that is neccessary for you to maintain an account with us, should you choose to login. You data stays on our servers and is never sold or given to third parties. See our privacy policy for more information.

## How do I delete my account?

For now, please send us an email with the email address associated with your Google Account and we can remove all your data from our servers. Please note this will not remove your Google Account.
